Dr Mukesh Kumar Soni: Best Urologist in Patna

Dr Mukesh Kumar Soni did his MBBS from Mahatma Gandhi Memorial Medical College and Hospital (MGMMCH), Jamshedpur.
Thereafter Dr Mukesh Kumar Soni pursued his Superspeciality MCh Degree in Urology from Vijaynagar Institute of Medical Sciences, Bellary.
Addinally Dr Mukesh Kumar Soni is Board Certified in Minimally Invasive Surgery popularly known as Laproscopic Surgery.
Dr Mukesh Kumar Soni has worked as Senior Resident at IGIMS Patna. He has also served as Assistant Professor at NMCH, Sasaram.
He was consultant Urologist at Ruban Memorial Hospital Patna.
He is a visiting consultant at Sri Sai Hospital, Sahyog Hospital, and Savera Hospital in Patna.
Having performed thousands of laproscopic surgeries, Dr Mukesh Kumar Soni is considered the foremost authority in laparoscopic stone removal surgeries in Patna and Bihar.
