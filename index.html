    About Us

    M.S Uro-Gynae & Stone Centre was started by Dr. Mukesh Kumar Soni and Dr. Rimjhim Kumari in 2017 and its new hospital building was inaugurated in Jan 2022. Dr Mukesh Kumar Soni and Dr. Rimjhim Kumari in their 20 years of practice across various private and governmental hospitals throughout India saw first hand how good treatement was becoming prohibitively expensive for common people. This catalyzed in them thier Doctoral instincts and thus M.S Uro-Gynae & Stone Centre was born to provide world class Urology and Gynaecology treatement at affordable budget.

    Our urologist and gynaecologists are awarded Best Infertility Specialist in Patna by MP Ravi Shankar Prasad.

    Our Urologist and Gynaecologist are trained in traditional and laproscopic surgery if there is any need of surgical intervention.

    • Insurance(TPA)/ Cashless: We have in house TPA and tie ups with all major Insurance providers for cashless treatment.
    • Ayushman Bharat: We are empanelled hospital to provide treatment free of charge to Ayushman card holders under PMJAY Jan Arogya scheme
    • In house Pharmacy, Diagnostic Centre and Sonography: We are well equipped to handle all scenarios in order to get you healthy.

    Our operation theateres are equipped with HD camera systems, C-Arm Image intensifier, Thallium Fibre laser, normal and flexible scopes and Anesthesia machines. Our hospital also has in house Diagnostic center for blood assays and Ultrasound (USG). Additonally we operate emergengy trauma center 24x7 for newborn deliveries and other emergencies. Ambulance service is also available.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Arming yourself with right information is good for health.

    Is Laparoscopy surgery done here?

    Yes, the hospital offers laparoscopic surgery. Procedures such as Pyeloplasty, Nephrectomy, Cholecystectomy (Lap Chole), Appendectomy, and Hernia Repair are available.

    How to practice Birth Control?

    Various birth control methods are available, and doctor can guide you based on your specific needs. Methods can include contraceptive pills, IUDs, and other hormonal methods to help you manage family planning effectively.

    How to get abortion?

    The hospital provides medicinal abortion (typically in the early stages of pregnancy) and surgical termination when required. Please consult with the doctor to understand the options based on your medical situation.

    How to get Pregnant?

    The hospital offers various fertility treatments including IVF (In Vitro Fertilization), IUI (Intrauterine Insemination), and ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection) to assist couples looking to conceive. Surrogacy, egg and sperm donation, and egg banking are also available.

    How to treat Kindney and Gallblader Stones?

    Treatments for Kidney Stones and Gallbladder Stones include stone removal procedures. Kidney stones are treated with techniques that break down or remove the stones, and gallstones may require surgical removal of the gallbladder (Cholecystectomy).

    Is Laser Surgery done here?

    Yes Dr Mukesh Kumar Soni has performed thousands of laser stone removal procedures. M.S Uro-Gynae & Stone Centre is considered the best laser stone removal clinic in Patna.
